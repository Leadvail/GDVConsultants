Workshop Overview

Format: 1-Day Intensive In-Person Workshop (On-site or Off-site)

Audience: Operational Directors, Site Managers, Crisis Teams, Shift Leads.

Capacity: Up to 12 delegates per session (to ensure coaching depth).

Key Learning Outcomes:

  • Understanding decision-making processes: The theory behind decision-making and the importance of risk versus benefit.
  • Combatting "Command Loneliness": How to hold the weight of responsibility without cracking under pressure.
  • Situational Awareness: How to obtain, understand and use information - and the factors that can affect this.
  • Human Factors: Understanding why your brain wants to take shortcuts (heuristics) and how to work with them.
